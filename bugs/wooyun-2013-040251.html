0x01:任意文件遍历漏洞

http://ffp.shenzhenair.com/ffp/.svn/entries

http://ffp.shenzhenair.com/ffp/CVS/Entries



0x03: fck 文件上传

http://ffp.shenzhenair.com/ffp/fckeditor/editor/filemanager/browser/default/browser.html?Type=Image&Connector=http://ffp.shenzhenair.com/ffp/fckeditor/editor/filemanager/browser/default/connectors/jsp/connector
